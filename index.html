The Strength in Truth

The Man Who Gave Too Much

Bobby spent years believing that love meant giving everything away. Living on the streets with little more than a monthly check and a worn Bible, he gave until kindness became exhaustion and compassion became pain. Through truth, loss, friendship, and unexpected grace, Bobby discovers that healthy boundaries are not selfish. Real love requires wisdom.
