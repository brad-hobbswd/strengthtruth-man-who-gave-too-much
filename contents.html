The Strength in Truth

The Man Who Gave Too Much

Contents
Chapter One: The Streets and His Heart Bobby gives endlessly to others while quietly carrying fear, loneliness, and a need to feel needed. Chapter Two: The Price of a Good Heart Raymond forces Bobby to confront a painful truth about helping and being used. Chapter Three: A Friend Named Grace A gentle stranger named Grace begins teaching Bobby that wisdom belongs beside compassion. Chapter Four: The Mirror of Mercy Old wounds and memories reveal why Bobby has spent years giving away pieces of himself. Chapter Five: The Gift of Boundaries Bobby learns that saying no can become an act of love and healing. Chapter Six: When Grace Fades Grace disappears from Bobby's daily life, leaving lessons that begin growing inside him. Chapter Seven: A Winter Test Winter brings Raymond back into Bobby's life and tests everything he has learned. Chapter Eight: The Shelter Within Bobby discovers peace, purpose, and the strength to help others wisely. Epilogue: Full Circle Years later, Bobby discovers how Jesus transformed pain into mercy and a shelter for others.
